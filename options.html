Button bindings

Press a button on your controller to highlight its row. Pick an action from the dropdown, choose Custom key press…, or choose Open specific tab… and enter a URL.

Combo bindings

Use two or more controller buttons together for a separate action. For example, add a combo with LB (L1) + RB (R1) and set it to New tab, or set it to Open specific tab… with netflix.com.

Analog sticks fixed

  • Left stick — scroll the page (vertical & horizontal)
  • Right stick — move the virtual cursor (slight tilt = precise, full tilt = fast)
  • Click with the button bound to Click (virtual cursor) — default R3 (press the right stick)
  • On-screen keyboard — default L3 toggles it. While open, the controller drives the keyboard: sticks/D-pad move the selection, A presses, B backspace, X space, Y shift, Start enter, Select closes
